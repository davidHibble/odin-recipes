Grilled Cheese

Description

While not directly related to Alfredo, Grilled cheese is another delicacy that many non-degenerate Americans enjoy.

The ones who don't are the same ones that like pineapple on pizza.

Ingredientes

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps for Preparation

  1. Take out two slices of bread
  2. Spread butter onto both bread slices, on both sides of each slice
  3. Place bread on skillet
  4. Let cook for 2-3 minutes
  5. Flip onto other side and let cook for additional 2 minutes
  6. Remove from skillet
